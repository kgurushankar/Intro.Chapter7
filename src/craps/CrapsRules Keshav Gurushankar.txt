Rules:
At the beginning of the game...
	If the player rolls a  7 or 11, the player automatically wins. 
	If the player rolls a 2, 3, or 12, the player automatically loses
	If the player rolls anything else, the points are set to the number rolled and the game continues.
During the game...
	If the player rolls a number that is the same number as the number of points, the player wins.
	If the player rolls a 7, the player loses. 
	If the player rolls anything else, the player keeps rolling until they get a 7 or the number that is the same number of points
